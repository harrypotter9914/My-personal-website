Babel

Built with a custom game engine

"Babel" is a 2D action-adventure game set in the mid-15th century in the Kingdom of Sicily. Amid the Black Death and famine, players explore the legendary Tower of Babel, battling enemies and uncovering mysteries of faith, power, and human ambition. The game combines Metroidvania mechanics with deep narrative elements, allowing players to unlock abilities like Shockwave, Wall Jump, and Crystal Dash to progress through complex levels and face tough bosses.

The driver has a unique gift: in the moment of impact, he can shift the accident into a timeless space, becoming a spirit who can freely investigate the scene. Handwritten Chinese Character Recognition System

Developed in Python, this system efficiently recognizes handwritten Chinese characters using image processing and neural networks. It supports image and direct handwriting inputs, processes them through advanced algorithms for noise reduction and sharpening, and uses a deep learning model for accurate character identification. The output includes the recognized text and an optional voice playback, catering to diverse application needs.

The Pawn

In the dark world of crime, an undercover cop and a vengeful assassin play a deadly game of revenge and redemption. The cop, posing as a loyal hitman to a powerful crime boss, secretly works for the police to dismantle the criminal empire. The assassin, driven by the pain of losing her parents in a gang war, manipulates the cop to exact her own revenge. As the story unfolds, alliances are tested, and betrayals change the course of their deadly game. In the end, the cop realizes he’s been nothing more than a pawn in the assassin’s masterfully executed plan. Chess Pieces is a thrilling tale of strategy, betrayal, and vengeance.

Impact of COVID-19 on Job Hunting and Employment for Beijing College Graduates

This video explores how the COVID-19 pandemic has affected job-seeking behaviors and employment outcomes for graduates from Beijing's undergraduate universities. Through a combination of data analysis and survey research, it highlights shifts in employment stability, increased emphasis on professional alignment, and transitions to digital job-seeking methods. The findings provide insights into how graduates, universities, and governments can adapt to promote higher-quality employment in the post-pandemic era.

The Evolution of China Under the CPC

This video chronicles the transformation of China under the leadership of the Communist Party of China (CPC) from its founding in 1921 to the present. It showcases key historical milestones such as the establishment of the CPC, the Long March, the founding of the People's Republic of China, and the economic reforms starting in 1978. Through historical footage and narrative, the video celebrates the CPC's role in shaping modern China and highlights the country's progress towards rejuvenation and modernization.
