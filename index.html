About Me

Hi there! I’m Yibo Wang, a game designer and developer who bridges game design and technical implementation to create immersive experiences.

I craft unique game mechanics and narratives as a designer, while also building the systems that bring them to life as a programmer—from Unity-based 2D platformers to Unreal Engine-powered emotional driving simulators.

When I’m not coding or sketching level layouts, you’ll find me flying drones to capture landscapes that inspire my game worlds, or collaborating with others—whether teaching science through interactive games or co-developing AI tools with international peers at Oxford’s summer program.

I believe the best games are born from teamwork and diverse perspectives, Let’s make magic together!

Research & Innovation

Portable Blood Pressure Monitor Based on Deep Learning

This project focuses on designing a smart bracelet for non-invasive blood pressure monitoring using PPG signals and deep learning algorithms. By integrating LSTM models and Bluetooth transmission, the device provides real-time health tracking, displaying results on a mobile app. The system enhances accessibility to blood pressure monitoring, offering a portable, efficient, and cost-effective solution for users.

Learn More

Auto-generation of certain featured live models based on Stable Diffusion and THA3

This project focuses on generating animated avatars using Stable Diffusion and THA3 to create customizable, motion-capturable 2D characters. By refining AI-generated images with background removal and structural adjustments, we enable seamless avatar animation. The goal is to make high-quality virtual hosts more accessible, reducing production costs for streaming, gaming, and digital entertainment.
