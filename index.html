Fit to Pass

Built with Unity

As an overworked office worker neglecting your health, you are abducted by a mysterious organization and turned into a prototype with the power to manipulate your body’s size and shape. To escape, you must master these abilities, navigate through challenging levels, and overcome obstacles, learning the importance of physical well-being along the way.

Babel

Built with a custom game engine

"Babel" is a 2D action-adventure game set in the mid-15th century in the Kingdom of Sicily. Amid the Black Death and famine, players explore the legendary Tower of Babel, battling enemies and uncovering mysteries of faith, power, and human ambition. The game combines Metroidvania mechanics with deep narrative elements, allowing players to unlock abilities like Shockwave, Wall Jump, and Crystal Dash to progress through complex levels and face tough bosses.

Echo of Crash

Built with Unreal Engine

On a peaceful morning, a family sets out for a drive, but their journey is interrupted by multiple accidents caused by careless drivers. The driver has a unique gift: in the moment of impact, he can shift the accident into a timeless space, becoming a spirit who can freely investigate the scene. He must uncover the truth behind each crash, helping others realize their mistakes and gradually restoring safety to the roads.
